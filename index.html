Privacy Policy for DhipyCare

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

At DhipyCare, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal, health, and financial information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, address.
  • Health Data: Medical history, symptoms, uploaded prescriptions.
  • Financial Information: Payment-related details (used for transactions only).
  • Location: For nurse/compounder visit coordination.
  • Cookies: Used to enhance user experience and track usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to:

  • Provide healthcare services (e.g., scheduling compounders or nurses)
  • Manage appointments and support communication
  • Ensure secure payments
  • Improve our platform and services

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Your health information is only shared with healthcare providers (compounders/nurses) involved in your care, under confidentiality agreements.

4. Security Measures

We use encryption, secure servers, and authentication protocols to protect your personal and health data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as needed to provide services and as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data anytime by contacting us.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at dhipycare@gmail.com.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.
